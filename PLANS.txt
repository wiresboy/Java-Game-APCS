• Side - scrolling platformer
• 16 x 16 pixel tile format
• 2-person multiplayer co-op over web-host
• Multiple different characters to choose from
• Level editor?
• No lives; single hit death (like Mario)
• On death, that particular player is sent back to their spawn point
• All players must reach the end of a level to continue on to next level
